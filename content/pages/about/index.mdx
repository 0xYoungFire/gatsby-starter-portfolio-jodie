## About

Otter Town was founded by avid investors who found a purpose under the warm glow of the DeFi summer, sharing a common interest - to make blockchain data accessible and usable. The team comprises of senior software engineers and software developers to navigate the complexities of the blockchain. We also have experienced finance experts and data analysts to provide easy to understand and actionable analytics to support DYOR and investment decision making.
